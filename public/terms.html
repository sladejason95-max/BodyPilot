Use of BodyPilot

BodyPilot helps users log food, training, check-ins, recovery, schedule events, coach messages, and physique-prep decisions. Users are responsible for the accuracy of information they enter and for using the app lawfully and safely.

Health and safety scope

BodyPilot is not medical advice, diagnosis, treatment, emergency guidance, or an automated prescribing or dosing system. Do not rely on the app as a substitute for a qualified physician, registered dietitian, licensed clinician, pharmacist, or emergency service.

Users should consult qualified professionals before changing training, nutrition, supplement, medication, hormone, or compound protocols. In an emergency, contact local emergency services.

Accounts, deletion, and backups

The current version supports local account records and local workspace data. Users can export a backup, reset local data, and initiate account deletion from in-app Settings.

Payments and subscriptions

No in-app purchases are sold in this build. If paid digital coaching features or subscriptions are introduced in a future App Store version, purchase terms, renewal, cancellation, and billing details must be shown clearly before purchase.

Support

For account, privacy, billing, or safety questions, contact support@bodypilot.app.
